05 — How I Work
My Editing Process

From raw footage to final reel — my step-by-step workflow.

01

Brief & Concept

Understand the content goal, target audience, and platform. Use AI tools (ChatGPT / Gemini / Claude) to refine the concept, hook, and structure.

02

Rough Assembly

Import raw footage into DaVinci Resolve. Build the rough cut — select the best clips, establish pacing, sync to audio/music.

03

Color & Motion

Apply color grade in DaVinci's Color page. Add transitions, motion graphic overlays via Panzoid, and any AI-assisted highlight clips via Swish AI.

04

Polish & Deliver

Add captions, adjust audio levels, do a final review pass. Export in platform-optimized settings (9:16, H.264, correct bitrate for Instagram).
